Greenock Morton 4-1 Alloa Athletic

  • 19 Mar
  • From the section Football

Declan McManus scored twice as Greenock Morton beat Scottish Championship bottom side Alloa Athletic, who remain 10 points adrift.

McManus netted from close range on the quarter-hour mark to open the scoring and Ross Forbes' deflected strike quickly doubled Morton's lead.

A shot by Kyle McAusland pulled Alloa back into the game before half-time.

But another McManus finish and one by Denny Johnstone late on ensured a comfortable win for the hosts.
